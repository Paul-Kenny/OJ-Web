Keith Darling

Vice President of Global Account Management

Keith Darling is VP Global Account Management, with responsibility for defining and executing the strategy to successfully manage the relationship between the OpenJaw Technologies and its global customers. Prior to this role, Keith was VP Managed Services at OpenJaw. Keith has 30 years’ experience in the airline and travel technology industry, having joined OpenJaw from Planitas Airline Systems where he was Chief Operating Officer. Prior to that Keith served as Head of Delivery at Datalex. Keith is a graduate of College of Marketing, Dublin.
