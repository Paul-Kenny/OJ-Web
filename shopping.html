Independent flight shopping for airlines

The OpenJaw t-Retail platform provides an advanced and customisable Booking Engine for airlines looking to sell more online. No matter what the Airline business model, cost savings are more important today than ever before. That’s why the t-Retail Platform uniquely supports all aspects of Flight Shopping including taxes and surcharges, without sending messages to external systems, thereby saving on expensive messaging costs. To achieve retailing excellence, the t-Retail Platform offers real-time business logic control, an ATPCO compliant fares pricing engine, worldwide multi-carrier inventory, Fare Family branding, online/offline payment options and lot more advanced features to drive online sales.

Personalise

Profiling engine enables the identification of the customer as an individual. Apply unique knowledge of customer preferences, behaviour and purchase history to anticipate what they are looking for. Use any element of customer data to determine what products to offer, for example: customer preferences, purchasing history, social and product browsing history, loyalty level, current session and shopping cart.
The industry’s leading rules engine can use these elements to determine the products to offer, their sort / bias order, price adjustments i.e. offer “the right product at the right time”.
Connect with customers across a range of channels including customer contact centres (for servicing of customer bookings), agency call centre sales and post-booking communications.

Differentiate

By using the t-Retail Platform, airlines are in full control, so differentiate the way you want and offer unique products that your competitors will not have.
The t-Retail Platform provides unrivalled business model control through the industry’s most powerful business rules engine. This means Airline Business Managers can apply and manage business logic without the need for programming. It also allows for much finer system control than what other vendors can offer.
Complete saving on Airline’s bill for fares shopping charges on GDS (Global Distribution Systems). Full ATPCO Fares and Rules implementation to facilitate accurate pricing. Therefore there is no dependency on GDSs or SITA for Fares Pricing
Updates / New Fares are made available on the Booking Engine within minutes of receiving the published updates from ATPCO. Provides much higher yield during promotions.
What’s more, you have access to worldwide flight inventory (availability), published and private fares support. You can create and manage branded fares (Fare Families) through the platform.
Additionally sell codeshare, interline and SPA carrier flights.
