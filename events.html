Events

OpenJaw regularly presents on travel retailing topics at industry events. We also regularly demo the latest developments in the t-Retail Platform. Book your meeting with us at our next event.

Aviation Festival Asia 2016

Singapore, 23rd – 24th February 2016

Catch up on the next-generation merchandising strategies shaping airline revenue generation, by listening to Paul Byrne, SVP Development, OpenJaw Technologies, present on the topic “The AB and NDC of Next Generation Air Merchandising” at Aviation Festival Asia. From A la carte ancillaries and Branded fares, to IATA NDC distribution, this presentation will uncover the full picture of opportunity presented by the new merchandising frontier. The presentation will take place at 11.00AM in the Seminar Theatre on 23 February. Also join Paul in a Round Table discussion taking place at 16.30 on 23 February, which will examine if Asian airlines really are maximizing their merchandising potential. Alternatively, stop by Stand 11 at Aviation Festival Asia to meet the OpenJaw team.
Book your 1-2-1 meeting by emailing: marketing@openjawtech.com.

New Generation PSS

London, 29th – 30th September 2015

Now in it’s second year, New Generation of Passenger Systems conference is dedicated to exploring the future of airline passenger systems which is a vital component of the airline industry currently. OpenJaw Technologies will be taking part in a panel discussion, as well as a sponsor of the event.

World Low Cost Airline Congress

London, 29th – 30th September 2015

We are happy to announce that OpenJaw Technologies are sponsoring this year’s World Low Cost Airlines Congress which takes place from 15-16 September 2015.
Mark Lenahan, VP Product Strategy, OpenJaw Technologies, will be moderating a panel discussion, “Increasing ancillary revenues through data, connectivity and a more retail focused mind-set?”.
Session panellists include:
Anna Chomse, Industry Head, Travel, Google
Glen Richardson, Chief Marketing Officer, Fruugo.com
Onur Dedeköylü, Vice President – Marketing, Pegasus Airlines
Mark will also be hosting a round table workshop, “Maintaining the customer experience both online and on board“.

IATA NDC Regional Workshop

Geneva, 1st – 2nd July 2015

In a series of regional workshops organized by IATA, airlines and travel technology vendors will meet to discuss progress, exchange ideas and learn about the latest NDC developments. Paul Byrne, SVP Development, will be attending.

Digital Travel Summit Europe

London, 17th – 18th June 2015

Digital Travel Summit focuses on how Europe’s leading hotels, airlines and OTAs can personalize customer engagement, leverage data analytics, capitalize on mobile and optimize their ecommerce platforms. Attend to discuss best practice strategies and practical changes for transforming your digital strategy and driving online revenue growth. Tony McQuillan, Product Manager, OpenJaw Technologies, will be attending.

SITA Air Transport IT Summit

Now in its 16th year, the SITA Air Transport IT Summit is the No. 1 forum for air transport IT leaders. This year we will be exploring the needs of connected travellers in an increasingly ‘always on’ aviation world.

OpenJaw t-Retailing Summit and User Conference 2015

Co. Kildare, Ireland, 19th – 21st May 2015

The dates of the OpenJaw t-Retailing Summit and User Conference have been announced. The event will take place from 19 – 21 May 2015 at Killashee House Hotel, Co. Kildare, Ireland. The OpenJaw t-Retailing Summit is an invitation only event which is open to industry professionals.

Technology & Innovation in Airline Distribution

London, 6th – 7th May 2015

OpenJaw will be sponsoring Technology and Innovation in Airline Distribution and Merchandising at the The Royal Garden Hotel, London, 6 – 7 May 2015. The event will seek to identify the key decisions and challenges in distribution and merchandising to push the debate forwards. Distribution and merchandising remains front and centre of the discussion on airline profitability – but to have an impact there are still significant business challenges to address… Join John McQuillan, co-founder, OpenJaw and Mark McDonald, Platform Development Manager – Digital Retail, Cathay Pacific Airways for a round table discussion on Omni-channel retailing for airlines, Wednesday 6 May at 15.30.
