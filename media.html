TravelSky Technology Acquires OpenJaw Technologies Limited

Total transaction value of US$39.4 million
DUBLIN, IRELAND – May 6, 2016 – TravelSky Technology Limited, the world’s third largest Global Distribution System and a leading provider of information technology solutions for China’s air travel and tourism industries, today announced that it has acquired OpenJaw Technologies, the leading online technology partner to the world’s biggest travel brands. The total transaction value is US$39.4 million (equivalent to approximately HK$305.6 million). Upon completion, OpenJaw becomes a wholly-owned subsidiary of TravelSky.
