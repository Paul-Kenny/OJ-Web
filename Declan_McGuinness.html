Declan McGuinness

Head of Sales

In his role as Senior Vice President of Sales, Declan is responsible for the global sales organisation at OpenJaw Technologies. Declan has built a wealth of experience in Enterprise Solutions Selling and Key Account Management through his many engagements with leading travel brands’ where he has helped them achieve their travel retailing objectives through the OpenJaw t-Retail Platform. Declan has been a career sales person since graduating from college. Having worked with USIT (the Student, Youth Independent Travel organisation), Declan held the position of Head of Sales for the Solutions and Consultancy Services Division of Ergo prior to joining OpenJaw. Declan graduated from University College Dublin with a degree in Social Sciences and also holds a H. Dip in International Selling from the Dublin Institute of Technology.
