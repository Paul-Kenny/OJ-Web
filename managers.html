Ricardo Navarro

Ricardo Navarro, Managing Director, OpenJaw Business Unit Ricardo Navarro is the Managing Director of OpenJaw. Ricardo is responsible for managing OpenJaw’s global operations and is an important voice on the senior leadership team for GuestLogix, providing a valuable perspective and expertise to the business as a whole. Ricardo was brought onboard to launch the OpenJaw Technologies business in Spain and Latin America in September 2005. He has since been the driving force behind the phenomenal growth of the region into the multi-million Euro business unit it is now, playing an integral role in building the Spanish-speaking business from the ground up and managing a successful team in Madrid. Before setting up OpenJaw’s first regional office, Ricardo worked in a variety of software development and project management roles for travel technology company Datalex and for Adquira Spain. Ricardo graduated from ICAI Comillas University with a Masters degree in Electronics and also holds an MBA from Escuela de Organización Industrial, both in Madrid.
